About ADR Trading

ADR Trading focuses on practical, real-world trading education. The goal is simple: help newer and developing traders understand risk, build a repeatable playbook, and move toward consistency — whether you’re trading stocks, options, or futures with a prop firm.

The approach is built around clear rules, patience, and accountability: not chasing signals, but understanding why you take each setup and how it fits into your overall plan.

What Makes This Different

  • Real setups based on structure, not random alerts.
  • Focus on risk management, daily loss limits, and staying funded.
  • Support for passing and keeping prop firm accounts.
  • Honest feedback and clear expectations for every student.
